About Qᵘᵃ Quiz

Welcome to Qᵘᵃ Quiz, your dedicated platform for generating custom objective questions for self-practice and exam preparation. Our mission is to provide an intuitive and effective tool for students and learners of all levels to test and reinforce their knowledge across a wide range of subjects.

We believe that active recall through practice questions is one of the most powerful learning techniques. Qᵘᵃ Quiz aims to make this process accessible and tailored to your specific needs, allowing you to focus on topics where you need the most improvement.

Our Purpose

Qᵘᵃ Quiz is designed with a clear educational purpose: to support and enhance your learning journey. We strive to offer a flexible tool that adapts to various study requirements, from quick topic reviews to comprehensive exam simulations.

Our core features include:

  • Instant generation of multiple-choice questions on any topic.
  • Customizable quiz length.
  • Immediate feedback on answers with detailed explanations.
  • Tracking of time taken for each quiz session.
  • Ability to download quizzes as PDF for offline study.
  • Offline functionality as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Our Vision

We envision Qᵘᵃ Quiz as a simple yet powerful companion for anyone committed to lifelong learning. We are continuously working to improve the question generation quality and add more features that contribute to a seamless and productive self-study experience.

Thank you for choosing Qᵘᵃ Quiz as part of your educational toolkit!
