Cookie Usage & Local Storage

Qᵘᵃ Quiz uses "cookies" and similar technologies (specifically, your browser's local storage) to enhance your experience and provide core app functionality.

1. What are Cookies and Local Storage?

  • Cookies: Small text files placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.
  • Local Storage: A feature in web browsers that allows web applications to store data persistently within the user's browser. Unlike cookies, data in local storage is not automatically sent to the server with every HTTP request, making it more efficient for storing larger amounts of client-side data.

2. How Qᵘᵃ Quiz Uses Local Storage (and Cookies)

Qᵘᵃ Quiz primarily relies on your browser's local storage to save your user preferences and progress directly on your device. This includes:

  • Your chosen theme (light/dark mode).
  • The questions generated in your current quiz session.
  • Your selected answers and quiz completion status.
  • Timer progress for your quiz.
This data is stored on your device so that when you reload the page or reopen the PWA, your previous session can be restored, providing a seamless experience. We do not access or collect this specific data from your device.

Third-Party Cookies: When Google AdSense is integrated, it may set its own cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing activity. These are third-party cookies and are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can often manage these preferences through your browser settings or Google's ad settings.

3. Your Choices

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies and local storage through their settings. You can typically set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, or you can choose to block cookies. However, please note that blocking or deleting local storage data may affect the functionality of Qᵘᵃ Quiz, particularly the ability to save your quiz progress and theme preference between sessions.

By continuing to use Qᵘᵃ Quiz, you consent to our use of local storage and, where applicable, cookies as described in this policy.
